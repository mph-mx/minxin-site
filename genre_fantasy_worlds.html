The Marvellers

Author: Dhonielle Clayton

Magic school pupil Ella finds herself the prime suspect for a crime she didn’t commit in this immersive and original dystopian magical fantasy. Uniquely thrilling and suspenseful, readers will be gripped by the richly imagined world in The Marvellers. This fast-paced first-in-a-series novel is perfect for all students . Highly recommended for fantasy lovers.

Genre: Fantasy Worlds

Reading Level: Explorer

Peregrine Quinn and the Cosmic Realm

Author: Ash Bond

Follow twelve-year-old Peregrine on a thrilling adventure as she discovers her godfather’s immortal tales are true. When portals between realms close, Daedalus disappears, prompting Peregrine and her new ally Rowan to search mystical places for him. The fast-paced middle-grade novel features relatable characters and offers valuable lessons on honesty and secrecy. An accessible and page-turning book, it will appeal to readers who enjoy fantasy, mystery and adventure. Highly recommended.

The Dark is Rising

Author: Susan Cooper

Just prior to his 11th birthday, Will discovers he’s the last person left with the power of the Old Ones, and his mission is to guard the Light and drive out the Dark. A gripping and imaginative story. The best-known (and stand-alone) book is actually the second book in the series chronology of a captivating series of titles for readers interested in fantasy and mystery. Read the first book if want to begin the sequence at the start.

Thirst

Author: Darren Simpson

Gorse grows up in Maimsbury, a place where the River Yeelde shapes tradition and daily life. Each spring the villagers give a farm animal to the water, believing it will keep the community prosperous. After a sudden tragedy, Gorse starts digging into the ritual and discovers that the river’s pact is far darker than anyone admits. Plant-based names mark who belongs and who doesn’t, and outsiders are steered away from the truth. Folklore, fear and buried history fuel this memorable and spectacularly compelling tale of sacrifice and survival that will hook teen readers.

The Balloon Thief

Author: Aneesa Marufu

When Khadija spots a hot air balloon about to break its moorings, she seizes a dangerous opportunity to flee from a life of arranged marriage.
