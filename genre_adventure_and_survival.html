Peregrine Quinn and the Cosmic Realm

Author: Ash Bond

Follow twelve-year-old Peregrine on a thrilling adventure as she discovers her godfather’s immortal tales are true. When portals between realms close, Daedalus disappears, prompting Peregrine and her new ally Rowan to search mystical places for him. The fast-paced middle-grade novel features relatable characters and offers valuable lessons on honesty and secrecy. An accessible and page-turning book, it will appeal to readers who enjoy fantasy, mystery and adventure. Highly recommended.
