Rugby Flyer

Author: Gerard Siggins

Rugby action collides with mystery sleuthing in this exciting adventure book series. When Eoin joins a prestigious rugby academy, he uncovers ghostly secrets and faces tough challenges both on and off the field. This sports-soaked thriller explores teamwork, friendship, and determination. Highly recommended for less confident readers.

Genre: Horror & Supernatural | Sport

Reading Level: Adventurer

The Haunting

Author: Margaret Mahy

A story of a haunting and a boy with a very special gift. A compelling and scary novel.

Genre: Horror & Supernatural

Reading Level: Adventurer

Thirst

Author: Darren Simpson

Gorse grows up in Maimsbury, a place where the River Yeelde shapes tradition and daily life. Each spring the villagers give a farm animal to the water, believing it will keep the community prosperous. After a sudden tragedy, Gorse starts digging into the ritual and discovers that the river’s pact is far darker than anyone admits. Plant-based names mark who belongs and who doesn’t, and outsiders are steered away from the truth. Folklore, fear and buried history fuel this memorable and spectacularly compelling tale of sacrifice and survival that will hook teen readers.

Genre: Fantasy Worlds | Horror & Supernatural

Reading Level: Voyager

Dracula

Author: Bram Stoker

A classic vampire tale following Dracula’s move from Transylvania to England, pursued by Abraham Van Helsing who is intent on exterminating him. Warning: this book may inspire your teen to wear black and visit Whitby. Ideal for discussion in Year 9.

Genre: Classics & Canon | Horror & Supernatural

Reading Level: Voyager

Frankenstein

Author: Mary Shelley

Mary Shelley’s gothic horror about a deranged scientist who wants to imagine his own living creation from dead bodies still has bite, shock value and complex themes. A well-written counterpoint to the myriad of recent zombie TV shows and computer games.

Genre: Classics & Canon | Horror & Supernatural

Reading Level: Voyager

The Lovely Bones

Author: Alice Sebold

Suzie is the victim of a brutal serial killer. Narrated by her spirit, this book tells the story of her life and her family in the past, present and future. This coming-of-age drama is imaginative and moving.

Genre: Horror & Supernatural | School, Family & Growing Up

Reading Level: Galactic
